Global Gift Guide

Every Holiday season, World Concern rolls out the Global Gift Guide. I designed the first version in 2009, and I redesigned it in 2015. I adapted the look and feel from the print version to the website to keep it unified. Since the guide was designed to be responsive and easy to use, it generated 20% more revenue than the year before.

Also, as a part of our Giving Tuesday campaign, I created a character named Totes Ma Goates, a charming goat that encouraged generous giving. We rolled out a whole marketing campaign: he had a jingle, Facebook ads, Tweets, and an email series. Because of this hair-brained idea, Giving Tuesday raised $54,000.

World Concern

In 2013, I led the redesign of World Concern’s website with a small but talented team consisting of a developer and a writer. We transformed the static website into a user-friendly, responsive experience, helping to solidify World Concern’s reputation as a trustworthy nonprofit. One of my primary objectives was to use rich imagery to help convey the story of World Concern and its beneficiaries. By telling visually compelling stories of transformation, the redesign helped to increase fundraising revenue by 14% and conversion rates by 123% year over year. Following the redesign, I’ve also been responsible for maintaining and upgrading the website for each new fundraising campaign. In 2017, I implemented a new donation platform that integrated with Salesforce.

Remember a Pet is a personalized pet sympathy card ordering service. Its primary audience is veterinary clinics. I was responsible for the visual design and front end development of the site. I worked with a software engineer and we built the site on top of Shopify. It was launched in March of 2016 and I’ve had the opportunity to work on and watch it grow.

Thanks for checking out my work. If you're interested in learning more, I'd love to hear from you. You can also find me on LinkedIn.

This site was built using SiteLeaf, Atom, iTerm, Sass, Github, Photoshop, and Typekit.
